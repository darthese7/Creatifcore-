## Project goal

Build a modern website for Creatif Core that introduces the brand, explains its offer clearly, showcases work or capabilities, and converts visitors into inquiries.

## Primary objectives

- Establish brand credibility.
- Present services or core offerings clearly.
- Showcase selected projects, work samples, or proof points.
- Drive contact, consultation, or quote requests.
- Deliver a polished experience across desktop and mobile.

## Proposed site map

1. Home
2. About
3. Services
4. Portfolio or Work
5. Contact
6. Optional: Blog, Insights, or Resources

## Recommended homepage structure

1. Header with navigation and primary call to action
2. Hero section with headline, supporting copy, and action button
3. Brand introduction or value proposition
4. Services overview
5. Featured work, case studies, or capabilities
6. Process or how we work
7. Testimonials, metrics, or trust signals
8. Final call to action
9. Footer with contact details and social links

## Content checklist

- Logo and brand assets
- Brand colors and typography direction
- Main headline and supporting copy
- About summary
- Service descriptions
- Project images or portfolio samples
- Testimonials or client proof
- Contact email, phone number, and social links

## Design direction

- Clean and modern visual system
- Strong typography with clear content hierarchy
- Creative but professional presentation
- Fast-loading, mobile-first layout
- Accessible color contrast and navigation patterns
